About me

My name is Cecilia (seh-see-lyah) and I’m a software engineer and UX/UI designer living in Córdoba, Argentina.

I’ve driven a user-centered approach throughout my entire career, whether it’s writing clear user manuals or designing the most effective and visually coherent screens, reports, presentations or documents, all while learning UX and improving my design skills. Additionally, my background working across various phases of the software development process has given me a thorough understanding of how software works, allowing me to develop solutions that are both usable and feasible.

I'm a team player, I contribute and encourage other to participate too, I believe everyone has great insights to share, we just need to listen.
